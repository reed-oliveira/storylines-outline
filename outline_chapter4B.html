Chapter 4

Best of a bad situation

How long has it been since that first night?

You sit upon an elaborate raft, waiting for the fish to bite. It’s so nice out here. The sun’s setting, a warm breeze blows through your hair. You glance at the shore, taking quick inventory of the traps you laid earlier. If you can’t catch any fish, at least you’ll have some meat for dinner.

Back at the house, you lay down your freshly caught game on the counter, along with some greens harvested from your farm. Nothing beats growing your own food, taming the wild, being your own person. Master of your own fate.

You look back on worse times, when you weren’t sure if help would ever come or if you would even survive those first few days. A smile takes your lips. A hoarse laugh forms in your throat. Of course you’d make it.

Life couldn’t be better.
