Chapter 4

Out of the woods

You almost fall out of your chair.

There was a car crash on the streets below. The commotion woke you up. You check the time. 2:35 PM. Groaning, you rub your eyes. Still tired. Still sleepy. Lunch must have been heavier than usual. You’re groggy and your mouth feels slimy.

With wobbly legs, you walk out of your office. One of your coworkers gives you a knowing smile, but doesn’t say anything. Is this the first time you fell asleep on the job? Probably not. Just keep walking.

The water cooler is in sight, and a quick drink fully wakes you up. Time to go back and finish the project you were working on... You can sleep later.
