Your Story

You found yourself in the middle of the woods in the dead of night. No recollection of yourself or how you got there. Coming upon a silver pond, you drank from its waters. It was refreshing, giving you enough energy and motivation to keep going. As you built your shelter and started a fire, the boon of the woods made itself manifest in the form of food, allowing you to thrive. Taming the woods and becoming master of your own fate, you survived and continue to thrive to this day.
