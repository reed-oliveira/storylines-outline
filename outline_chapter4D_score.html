Your Story

You found yourself in the middle of the woods in the dead of night. No recollection of yourself or how you got there. Thinking it may make rescuing efforts harder, you decide not to explore. Strange lights dance in the distance, beckoning you. Thinking it may just be a trap or a trick of the senses, you stay put and let time pass. Boredom sets in, and eventually you bury your feet in the cold, comfortable earth. The woods claim you as one of their own, and you cease rational thought, forsaking your own humanity. You are here forever.

Choices

You chose to stay put.

Chapter 2

Chapter 3
