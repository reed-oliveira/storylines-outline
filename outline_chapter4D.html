Chapter 4

Part of these woods

It’s been a long time.

You refused to move.

Refused to take action.

Refused to try.

In the end, it didn’t matter. You were fearful of death, and found something much worse instead.

Stasis.

The absence of motion. Of progress. Of action. An emptiness that the human mind can’t even comprehend.

Not that you need to worry about that anymore. You stopped worrying a long time ago. Stopped thinking.

You made the decision to not do anything. To bury your own two feet into the earth and create your own roots here.

These woods listened. They heard your request. They accepted it.

You’re theirs now. Just another tree in the forest.

Right where you belong.
